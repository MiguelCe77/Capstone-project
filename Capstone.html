Competitive Melee

An introduction

What is Melee?

Melee or Super Smash Bros. Melee is a fighting game made for the Nintendo Gamecube in 2001. The game features characters from Nintendo franchies and has them battle against eachother in stages.

Throughout its 15 year life span the game has lived on and has only been getting bigger in recent years. It is one of the most viewed games on twitch.tv (a livestreaming website) with over 200k viewers during big tournaments. The game has been played on huge stages like the Mandalay Bay in Las Vegas. Huge tournaments have over 2000 people entering and competing over 20,000 dollars. With the huge popularity of the game some players have gained over 200k dollars in earning from just winning tournaments.

How is a 15 year old Nintendo Party game played competitively?

That is what this website is for, to answer that question

Over the first years of the games lifespan, people began to take the game seriously. A community began to form as tournaments sprouted out of community members houses, basements, and rented venues. As members of the community took the game more seriously, advanced techniuques were found to advances the games skill ceiling. These advanced techniques were hidden from the casual fanbase of the game, but the competitive community fully embraced them and used them in their games. The game is so unique in the world of fighting games that the sequels of Melee (Brawl, Smash 4) did not kill the game's community off so the game lived throughout the years.
